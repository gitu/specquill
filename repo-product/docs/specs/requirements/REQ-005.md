# Conformant OKF bundles

Spec workspaces are knowledge for humans and agents alike; they SHALL remain
readable without SpecQuill.

> **REQ-005.1 · MUST** — Every document SHALL carry a `type` frontmatter
> field (OKF v0.1 conformance).

> **REQ-005.2 · SHOULD** — Opted-in workspaces SHALL regenerate `index.md`
> listings and the `log.md` change history at commit time, in the same
> commit as the change they describe. (Both files are optional in OKF v0.1 —
> "MAY appear in any directory" — generating them is a producer convenience.)

> **REQ-005.3 · MUST** — Generated links SHALL be relative markdown paths, so
> a bundle renders correctly on any forge without SpecQuill. The `/`-absolute
> form OKF recommends SHALL still be accepted when reading existing content.
