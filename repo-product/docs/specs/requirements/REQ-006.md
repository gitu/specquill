# Real-time collaborative editing

Markdown documents opened for editing SHALL support concurrent authors with
convergent, conflict-free results.

> **REQ-006.1 · MUST** — Editors of the same (branch, path) SHALL join one
> shared session and converge on the same document regardless of edit order
> (CRDT semantics).

> **REQ-006.2 · MUST** — The server SHALL act only as a relay and durable log
> of opaque updates; it SHALL NOT interpret document content to resolve
> conflicts.

> **REQ-006.3 · MUST** — While a collaborative session is live it OWNS the
> file: direct writes to that (branch, path) SHALL be rejected
> (`room_active`), and background branch updates SHALL be withheld.

> **REQ-006.4 · MUST** — A late joiner SHALL receive the full history and
> reach the current state before its own edits are accepted.
