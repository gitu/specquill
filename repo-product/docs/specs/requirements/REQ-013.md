# Verifiable link integrity

Every link in a workspace SHALL be verifiable on demand, with link classes
checked by their nature.

> **REQ-013.1 · MUST** — Internal links (any authored style) SHALL be checked
> against the branch's file set.

> **REQ-013.2 · MUST** — Cross-repo `~source/…` links SHALL be checked against
> the granted source's default branch; an ungranted or unselected source is a
> finding, never an access path.

> **REQ-013.3 · MUST** — External http(s) links SHALL be probed server-side
> with bounded time and count; probes SHALL refuse private addresses, and
> external checking SHALL be optional per run.

> **REQ-013.4 · SHOULD** — Results SHALL separate the classes and point back
> to the linking document.
