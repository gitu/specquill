# Self-describing, extensible entity model

The document families a workspace is made of SHALL be visible, explained and
extensible — not an implicit folder convention.

> **REQ-012.1 · MUST** — Every entity family SHALL carry a user-facing
> description, shown where users encounter the family (model view, tree).

> **REQ-012.2 · MUST** — Workspaces SHALL be able to define custom entity
> families in the in-repo config (folder, label, icon, color, description),
> and to override the presentation of built-in families.

> **REQ-012.3 · MUST** — Documents in folders outside any configured family
> SHALL still be browsable — never silently hidden.

> **REQ-012.4 · SHOULD** — Files created in a custom family SHALL receive the
> family's document `type` in their frontmatter.
