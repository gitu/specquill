# Grounded AI copilot

The copilot SHALL answer from the actual workspace and its granted reference
sources, and its authority to change files SHALL be bounded.

> **REQ-007.1 · MUST** — The copilot context SHALL be built from the current
> branch snapshot plus the project's effective, grounded references; content
> the workspace does not contain SHALL NOT be asserted as fact.

> **REQ-007.2 · MUST** — Grounded reference files SHALL appear as read-only
> context under `~<source>/<path>` headings, and the writable workspace SHALL
> retain a floor share of the context budget under reference pressure.

> **REQ-007.3 · MUST** — Copilot draft edits SHALL only target writable
> project files; any `~`-prefixed (reference) path SHALL be refused.

> **REQ-007.4 · MUST** — Draft edits SHALL land as uncommitted changes on a
> dedicated branch for human review, never as direct commits to a protected
> branch.
