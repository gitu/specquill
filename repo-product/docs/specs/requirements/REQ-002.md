# Byte-fidelity editing

Documents belong to their authors; the tool must never rewrite what it did
not change.

> **REQ-002.1 · MUST** — Saving an untouched document SHALL produce a
> byte-identical file.

> **REQ-002.2 · MUST** — Frontmatter edits SHALL preserve key order,
> comments and formatting of untouched entries.

> **REQ-002.3 · SHOULD** — Rich-text edits MAY normalize the markdown of the
> edited document to house style; normalization SHALL only occur after a
> real user edit.
