# Unauthenticated OKF-bundle share links

A project SHALL be downloadable as a single OKF-bundle zip through a URL
whose secret token is the only credential — so the bundle can be pasted into
an LLM chat or fetched by an agent with no login.

> **REQ-016.1 · MUST** — Minting, rotating and revoking a share link SHALL
> require the maintainer role on the project ([REQ-021](REQ-021.md)) — the
> protected-content rung. Downloading SHALL require nothing beyond the
> token in the URL.

> **REQ-016.2 · MUST** — The bundle SHALL be built from the project's
> DEFAULT branch only. Drafts, workspace branches and uncommitted state
> SHALL never be reachable through a share link.

> **REQ-016.3 · MUST** — The bundle SHALL be the complete project content —
> binaries and the `.specquill/` files included — scoped to the project's
> content root with project-relative paths.

> **REQ-016.4 · MUST** — At most one link SHALL be active per project.
> Re-minting SHALL rotate the token; rotation and revocation SHALL
> invalidate the previous link immediately.

> **REQ-016.5 · SHOULD** — Tokens SHALL be unguessable (≥128 bits of
> entropy) and responses marked non-cacheable.
