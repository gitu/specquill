# Guided document creation with collision-free IDs

Creating a document SHALL be a guided act — pick a family, a location and a
title — never the typing of a raw file path. Identifiers SHALL be generated
from a per-family scheme and be unique across the workspace.

> **REQ-015.1 · MUST** — Creation SHALL offer the workspace's document
> families (built-in and custom entities) with their descriptions, and place
> the file under the family's folder. Existing subfolders SHALL be offered;
> new subfolders — nested ones included — SHALL be creatable inline.

> **REQ-015.2 · MUST** — Each family SHALL have an ID scheme: configured in
> `.specquill/config.yml` under `ids:`, else a built-in default. Schemes are
> token patterns (sequential numbers, random digits/hex, memorable words,
> year, title slug).

> **REQ-015.3 · MUST** — Generated IDs SHALL be checked for conflicts against
> existing file names and frontmatter `id:` values across the workspace,
> case-insensitively. Sequential tokens SHALL continue after the highest
> matching existing ID; random tokens SHALL re-roll around collisions.

> **REQ-015.4 · MUST** — A conflicting ID (generated or hand-edited) SHALL
> block creation visibly; it SHALL never overwrite an existing document.

> **REQ-015.5 · SHOULD** — Where a scheme derives from the title, the ID
> SHALL track title edits until the author takes over the ID field; an
> untitled document SHOULD receive a memorable placeholder, not a generic
> name.
