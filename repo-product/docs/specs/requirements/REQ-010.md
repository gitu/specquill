# Portable diagrams and sketches

Diagrams embedded in specs SHALL remain viewable and editable without lock-in
to a proprietary format.

> **REQ-010.1 · MUST** — Sketches SHALL be stored as PNG images with the
> editable scene embedded, so they render natively anywhere a PNG does.

> **REQ-010.2 · MUST** — Opening an embedded sketch SHALL recover its editable
> scene; re-saving SHALL preserve edits round-trip.

> **REQ-010.3 · SHOULD** — Text-based diagrams (e.g. Mermaid) SHALL be
> authored inline in markdown and rendered on view.
