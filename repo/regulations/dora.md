# DORA

Digital Operational Resilience Act — ICT risk and incident reporting.

## Art. 19 — Major ICT incident reporting {#art-19-incident-reporting}
Financial entities shall report major ICT-related incidents to the relevant competent
authority within the regulatory deadlines (initial, intermediate, final).
