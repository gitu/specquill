# GDPR

General Data Protection Regulation — obligations relevant to reporting data.

## Art. 5 — Storage limitation {#art-5-storage-limitation}
Personal data shall be kept for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which
it is processed. Reporting records retain a **7-year** window (regulatory override).

## Art. 17 — Right to erasure {#art-17-erasure}
Erasure requests must reconcile against the regulatory retention hold on transaction
reports. Drives: [REQ-090 · Data Retention](../requirements/REQ-090.md).
