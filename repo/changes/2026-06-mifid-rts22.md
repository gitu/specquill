# MiFID II RTS 22 — microsecond timestamp precision

## What changed
Execution timestamps must be captured and reported to **microsecond** granularity
(previously: second).

## Impact (AI-assessed)
- **REQ-042** Transaction Reporting — needs update
- **specs/txn-report.md** — §Timing & precision
- **data-mappings/trade.md** — `trade.executionTimestamp` ⚠ drift
