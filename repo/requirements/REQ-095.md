# ICT Incident Reporting

Major ICT-related incidents MUST be reported to the competent authority within the DORA
deadlines (initial, intermediate, final).

> **REQ-095.1 · MUST** — An initial notification SHALL be issued within the regulatory
> window of classifying an incident as major.

- ⚖ **Regulatory** — [DORA · Art. 19](../regulations/dora.md#art-19-incident-reporting)
