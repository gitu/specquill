# Transaction Reporting

All reportable transactions executed on trading venues **MUST** be reported to an
Approved Reporting Mechanism (ARM) no later than the close of the following working
day (**T+1**). This requirement is driven jointly by the MiFID II RTS 22 mandate and
the internal T+1 settlement SLA agreed with operations.

> **REQ-042.1 · MUST** — The system SHALL transmit a complete, validated transaction
> report for every reportable event within one working day of execution, including all
> fields mandated by RTS 22 Annex I.

## Drivers
- ⚖ **Regulatory** — [MiFID II · RTS 22 Art. 26](../regulations/mifid-ii.md#rts-22-art-26)
- ◆ **Product** — Ops T+1 settlement SLA

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Report generated for 100% of reportable events
- [ ] Submitted to ARM before the T+1 cutoff (21:00 UTC)
- [x] Execution timestamp precision ≥ microsecond — *pending [CHG-2026-06](../changes/2026-06-mifid-rts22.md)*
