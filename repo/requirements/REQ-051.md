# Exception Handling

Reports that fail validation MUST be routed to an exceptions queue, triaged, corrected
and resubmitted within the T+1 window.

> **REQ-051.1 · MUST** — The system SHALL not silently drop a reportable event; every
> failed report SHALL raise an exception with a resolution owner.
