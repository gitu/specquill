# Data Retention

Transaction reports MUST be retained for the regulatory window (7 years) and reconciled
against GDPR erasure requests.

> **REQ-090.1 · MUST** — A regulatory retention hold SHALL take precedence over an
> erasure request for reportable records.

- ⚖ **Regulatory** — [GDPR · Art. 5 / Art. 17](../regulations/gdpr.md#art-5-storage-limitation)
